About Chiikawa Puzzle

Welcome to the Charming World of Chiikawa Puzzle!

Chiikawa Puzzle is a captivating memory game created by indie developer emptygamer as a loving tribute to Nagano’s beloved Chiikawa manga and anime series. Released in 2023, this fan-made gem lets you piece together adorable character faces like Chiikawa, Hachiware, and Usagi in a cozy, browser-based experience. The game’s unique twist? Once you place a puzzle piece, it disappears, challenging your memory to recall each position. Designed for fans and casual players alike, it’s simple to pick up but keeps you hooked with its clever mechanics. Play now at chiikawapuzzle.org or emptygamer.itch.io and immerse yourself in the kawaii world of Chiikawa!

Gameplay Overview

In Chiikawa Puzzle, your goal is to recreate the lovable faces of Chiikawa characters by placing puzzle pieces on a hidden board. The catch? Each piece vanishes once placed, forcing you to rely on memory to complete the puzzle. Whether you aim for perfection or enjoy creating quirky, mixed-up versions, the game offers endless fun with its bite-sized sessions. With intuitive touch or click controls, it’s accessible on both mobile and desktop, making it ideal for quick breaks or cozy gaming moments.

  • Build Cute Characters: Assemble Chiikawa, Hachiware, Usagi, and more.
  • Test Your Memory: Recall piece positions as they disappear after placement.
  • Play Your Way: Create perfect portraits or silly combinations for laughs.

How to Play Chiikawa Puzzle

Start the Game: Visit chiikawapuzzle.org or emptygamer.itch.io and click “Play Now” to begin instantly in your browser.

Choose a Character: Select a favorite Chiikawa character, like Chiikawa, Hachiware, or Usagi, each with unique puzzle layouts.

Place Puzzle Pieces: Click or tap to position pieces (e.g., eyes, ears) on the hidden board, guided by subtle visual cues.

Rely on Memory: Remember where each piece goes as they vanish upon placement, challenging your spatial awareness.

Complete or Experiment: Finish the character’s face accurately or mix pieces for fun, creative results.

Share Your Creation: Save and share your completed (or hilariously mixed-up) Chiikawa faces with friends on social media.
